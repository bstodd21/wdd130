Overview

Purpose

Hello, my name is Brayden Todd, and I am a computer programmer. My company develops applications that help people find the best prices on houses. Have you been stressed while looking for a house because of how much the housing market has skyrocketed? We are here to help take that stress off your shoulders. We want a website that will provide a list of houses no more than $400,000. We understand that paying for a house over that amount is ridiculous sometimes and with this website, we can help people feel confident that they are paying a good price and paying for a house they love.

Audience

We strive to provide the best prices for the most amazing and beautiful housing for everyone. Everyone loves a nice house, but the nicest house on the market can be over $400,000. We want to give our customers the opportunity to not stress about not being able to find their future home. Partnered with the best real estate agencies, we assure that you are getting the best price for the house you love. Live luxurious, but affordably!!
